Dining At Los Pollos

For a truly memorable dining experience, cuisine and atmosphere are paired as thoughtfully as food and wine. It is located on the 13th floor, offers a delightful menu of Chinese Hunan, Japanese, Thai and Korean cuisines. The delectable entrées combined with the picturesque panoramic views of the city and Gulshan Lake leaves you in complete reverie. Whether you dine inside, or on the balcony with the soft lake breeze, We promises to amaze you. In addition to the conventional kitchens for Hunan and Korean cuisines, our highly skilled chefs present and prepare selected entrées on a Teppanyaki grill.
